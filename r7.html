Using Meshmixer, I cut my face in half so make my head inside the workable area. After that, I sent it to the Milling machine, and then I waited. After I have the wax mold, I pourer some pink liquid (silicone?) into the mold and let it rest of a day. A day later, I removed the pink mold and it came out as a elastic pink mold. Then as a class we melted chocolate chips and poured it into our molds. We put our molds into the fridge for a day and then it came out as a chocolate head that is edible.










First things first I had to make the skateboard. I glued around 7 boards together at a specific order. Then I placed it in a vacuum bag with a board former. I sucked out all the air with a pump and the board began to shape. A day later, I made a sticker and printed it out on the VersaCamm. I placed the sticker on the back and on the front I laser engraved somethings.
