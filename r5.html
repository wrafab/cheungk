Week 5: 3D Printing/Scanning

. What are the design rules and limitations for the MakerBot Replicator 5th Gen printers?
Size is a big limitation for the MakerBot, but other than that, you can really make anything. There are certain limitations on how fine a #D printed object can be and the texture of the surface on the MakerSpace will take a long time. The triangular template is also a limitation for the STL file.
What type of 3D printing process does the MakerBot Replicator use?
The MakerBot uses the additive process.
What are the advantages to this type of printer versus the FormLabs printer?
The MakerBot printer is relatively cheaper. However, the FormLab printer is advanced in its detailed printing. The MakerBot can print larger objects and for the common use.
